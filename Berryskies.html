Manuscripts - Books that were written long ago by hand on palm leaf or a specially prepared
bark of a tree known as the birch. It was written in 3 different languages. Tamil,Sanskrit and Prakrit
(languages spoken by ordinary people.) These books dealt with all kinds of religious beliefs, the life of
kings,medicine and science etc. There were also plays or stories.

Inscriptions- Writings on relatively hard surfaces such as stone or metal.
Sometimes, kings got their orders inscribed so that the people could see the orders and follow them.

Hunter gatherers

Hunter gatherers were the earliest people. They hunted animals,caught fishes and birds and gathered roots,fruits,leaves,stalks,eggs
and other forest produce for their food. Hunter gatherers moved from place to place. There were many reasons for this movement:-
Reasons
In search of food. When hunter-gatherers had lived at a place for a long time,they would have eaten up all the available plant and animal resources They had to follow the movements of animals. Animals moved to different places in search of food. In search of water. They had to move from season to season in search of different kinds of plants.
